# Community Code of Conduct

The AI in Cyber Alliance is a community-first space. We are committed to providing a
friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone — regardless of experience level,
background, employer, identity, or affiliation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps build a positive community:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints, experiences, and skill levels
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive technical feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and its members
- Showing empathy and courtesy toward other attendees and speakers

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Personal or political attacks, or demeaning comments
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Using talks or discussion time for **product pitches or marketing** — our events are
  educational and experience-driven, not sales venues
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
  setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies at all Alliance events (in person and online), in our
community channels, and whenever an individual is representing the community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the organizers via
our [LinkedIn group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/17875019/). All reports will be
handled with discretion. Organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, up to
and including removal from an event and future events.
